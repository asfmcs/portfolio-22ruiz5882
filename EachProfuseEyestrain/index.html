Egg Hot Dog
1 egg 1 sausage
Salt Hot dog buns
Pepper Ketchup
Cooking Oil Mustard
Cooking Oil

Egg

  1. Spray pan with cooking oil.
  2. Crack egg open and fry in pan.
  3. Let egg cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Turn off stove and put egg into plate.

Hot Dog

  1. Spray pan with cooking oil.
  2. Cook sausage and toast the buns for about 5 minutes
  3. Turn off stove take out buns and sausage
  4. Put sausage inside of the buns and apply mustard and ketchup
